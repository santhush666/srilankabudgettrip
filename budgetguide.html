Top 10 Money Saving Hacks

Proven strategies to stretch your budget further

Use 2nd/3rd Class Trains

Experience the famous scenic trains at a fraction of the cost. 2nd class is comfortable, 3rd class is super cheap and authentic.

Save 60-80%

Stay in Homestays

Homestays are cheaper than hotels and often include breakfast. You get local interaction and authentic Sri Lankan hospitality.

Save 40-60%

Eat at Local "Hotels"

Small local restaurants called "hotels" serve delicious rice and curry for $1.50-3. Look for places filled with locals.

Save 70% on food

Free Hiking Alternatives

Instead of paying $30 for Sigiriya, hike Pidurangala Rock nearby for $3 with similar views. Many free hiking trails available.

Save $27 per person

Use PickMe App

Sri Lanka's version of Uber for tuk-tuks. Fixed prices prevent overcharging. Much cheaper than hailed tuk-tuks in tourist areas.

Save 30-50% on transport

SAARC Country Discounts

If you're from India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, etc., you get 50% off at most paid attractions. Always carry your passport.

Save 50% on attractions

Refill Water Bottles

Many guesthouses have filtered water. Buy one large bottle and refill it. Saves money and reduces plastic waste.

Save $1-2 daily

Travel Overnight

Take overnight buses or trains for long distances. Saves on accommodation and travel time. Book 2nd class sleeper for comfort.

Save $10-15 per night

Visit in Shoulder Season

April-May and September-October offer good weather with fewer crowds and lower prices. Avoid December-January peak.

Save 20-40% overall

Share Costs

Travel with a partner or make friends to share accommodation, transport, and sometimes even guide costs.

Save 25-40% as a pair
